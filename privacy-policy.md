# Privacy Policy for SOS Flash Signal

Effective date: 6-23-2026

SOS Flash Signal is designed as an offline utility app for SOS signaling, Morse code, flashlight control, screen light, and sound alerts.

## Information We Collect

SOS Flash Signal does not collect, sell, rent, or share personal information.

The app does not require an account and does not ask users to provide names, email addresses, phone numbers, location, contacts, photos, or payment information.

## Camera Permission

SOS Flash Signal may request camera permission only to control the device flashlight or torch.

The app does not take photos, record videos, access the camera feed, or store camera data.

## Notifications

SOS Flash Signal may request notification permission so widget-triggered SOS or flashlight actions can show an active notification with a Stop button.

Notifications are used only for app controls and safety feedback.

## Local Storage

SOS Flash Signal stores some information locally on the user’s device, such as:

* app settings
* saved Morse signals
* recent messages
* speed, repeat, sound, and output preferences

This information stays on the device and is not sent to a server.

## Internet Access

SOS Flash Signal does not require internet access for its core features.

## Data Sharing

SOS Flash Signal does not share user data with third parties.

## Children’s Privacy

SOS Flash Signal is not designed to collect information from children. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Safety Notice

Flashing lights may affect people who are sensitive to flashing or strobe effects. Do not aim the flashlight or screen flash at people, drivers, animals, or traffic.

SOS Flash Signal is a utility tool and should not replace official emergency services.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the app changes, especially if features such as ads, analytics, cloud backup, or online services are added.
