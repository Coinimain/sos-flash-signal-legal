## App Purpose

SOS Flash Signal is a utility app for SOS signaling, Morse code, flashlight control, screen light, and sound alerts.

The app is provided as a general utility tool and should not replace official emergency services.

## Safety

Users are responsible for using the app safely.

Do not point flashing lights at people, drivers, animals, aircraft, vehicles, or traffic. Flashing lights may affect people who are sensitive to strobe or flashing patterns.

## No Emergency Guarantee

SOS Flash Signal may help send visible or audible signals, but it does not contact emergency services, police, ambulance, fire services, or rescue teams.

In an emergency, contact your local emergency number directly.

## Device Limitations

Some features may not work on all devices. Flashlight behavior depends on device hardware, Android version, permissions, and manufacturer restrictions.
