# SOS Flash Signal Support

Thank you for using SOS Flash Signal.

SOS Flash Signal is a utility app for SOS signaling, Morse code, flashlight control, screen light, and sound alerts.

## Common Questions

### Why does the app request camera permission?

Camera permission is used only to control the phone flashlight. SOS Flash Signal does not take photos or record videos.

### Why does the app request notification permission?

Notification permission is used so widget-triggered SOS or flashlight actions can show an active notification with a Stop button.

### The flashlight does not work

Check that:

* camera permission is enabled
* your device has a flashlight
* no other app is currently using the camera or flashlight

You can still use screen flash and sound mode even if hardware flashlight is unavailable.

### The widget does not appear

Try opening the app once after installation, then check your phone’s widget picker again. Some launchers may require a restart before showing a newly installed widget.

## Safety Notice

Do not point flashing light at faces, drivers, animals, or traffic. Flashing lights may affect people sensitive to strobe or flashing patterns.
