# SOS Flash Signal

SOS Flash Signal is an Android utility app for SOS signaling, Morse code, flashlight control, screen light, and sound alerts.

Use the app to send quick visual or audible signals with your phone’s flashlight, screen, and speaker.

## Features

* Emergency SOS mode
* Morse code sender
* Flashlight signal output
* Screen light output
* Sound/beep output
* Saved signals
* Home screen widget controls
* Adjustable speed, repeat, and output settings

## Safety Notice

Flashing lights may affect people who are sensitive to strobe or flashing effects. Do not aim flashing lights at people, drivers, animals, aircraft, vehicles, or traffic.

SOS Flash Signal is a utility tool and does not contact emergency services. In an emergency, contact your local emergency number directly.
